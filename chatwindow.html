DATA POOL FOR FAMILIES

We understand today’s family is big on digital connectivity. The kids are glued to games on their tablets, Mum loves researching the next family holiday from the couch, while Dad can’t get enough sport streamed live to his device.

When using a Data Pool, you get the convenience of one bill and all the data inclusions become one big pool to share between all connected devices.

 

DATA POOL FOR ME

Are you one of the many out there who have multiple devices that all use heaps of data? All the social buzz on your mobile, catching up on TV via your tablet on the commute home – and that’s just the tip of your digital iceberg!

Data Pool gives you the convenience of one bill for all your eligible mobile plans, so that all the data inclusions of your connected devices become one.

 

WHAT’S GREAT ABOUT DATA POOL?

  • If you need extra data, we’ll automatically top you up for just $10 per extra 1GB for use in that billing month.
  • You can monitor your spend through the My Optus app and in Optus My Account. Awesome!
  • UNLIMITED standard national talk and text for everyone connected to one of our latest My Plan Plus (including SIM Only) mobile plans.
 

HOW DO I GET DATA POOL?

Simply connect any combination of the following plans onto the one bill, and the data inclusions will pool to share between all connected devices:

  • My Plan Plus mobile phone plans
  • My Plan Plus SIM Only mobile phone plans
  • My Mobile Broadband Plus plans

Already a customer: You can add additional services to an existing account. Just make sure all services are connected to eligible plans. Simply log in to Optus My Account and purchase online.

New customers: Shop online and purchase your desired eligible plans; we’ll do all the magic stuff behind the scenes to get you up and running.
